Ingredients list

  2. caster sugar

  4. plain flour
  5. baking powder
  6. jam

Procedure

Step1:
Creme the butter and sugar
Step 2:
Mix through the egg
Step 3:
Sift over the plain flour and baking powder
Step 4:
Roll the dough
Step 5:
Chill the biscuits
Step 6:
Bake the biscuits
